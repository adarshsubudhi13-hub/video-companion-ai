# 🧠 MindFluent AI

> Transform any video into easy-to-understand notes, summaries, quizzes, and study materials using AI.

🌐 **Live Demo:** https://mind-fluent-learn.lovable.app

---

## 📖 About

MindFluent AI is an AI-powered learning assistant that helps students, professionals, and lifelong learners learn faster from video content.

Simply upload a video or paste a video URL, and the AI automatically analyzes the content to generate structured notes, concise summaries, key insights, timestamps, quizzes, and memory-friendly study materials.

Instead of watching long videos repeatedly, users can quickly understand, revise, and retain the important concepts.

## 🚀 How It Works

1. Upload a video or paste a supported URL.
2. AI extracts and analyzes the video content.
3. Speech is converted into text.
4. The AI understands the context.
5. Structured notes and summaries are generated.
6. Important concepts and timestamps are identified.
7. Quiz questions are created for revision.
8. Users can review, learn, and export their notes.
